# Governance

## Current model: BDFN (benevolent dictator for now)

tracebloom is in pre-alpha. Decision-making rests with the current maintainer. This keeps the project moving fast during the six-month build to v1.0.

"For now" is load-bearing. The BDFN model is an explicit placeholder, not an end state.

## Decision-making

- **Technical direction and scope:** current maintainer decides, after reading the threads.
- **Code changes:** proposed via pull request; merged by a maintainer after review.
- **Locked decisions:** items recorded in `AGENTS.md` under "locked scope" do not get relitigated without a written RFC and a stated reason.

## Upgrade path: steering committee

When at least one of the following is true, the project will transition to a small steering committee:

1. Three or more regular contributors (ten+ merged PRs each) are active.
2. A commercial entity offers to fund the project in a way that creates a conflict-of-interest risk.
3. v1.0 ships and the community exceeds the current maintainer's capacity to decide alone.

The steering committee will be 3–5 people, odd number, drawn from active contributors. It will publish its decisions in this repository.

## Contributing

See `CONTRIBUTING.md` for how to propose changes, report bugs, and participate in reviews. The Code of Conduct (`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`) applies to all community spaces.

## Amending this document

This document may be amended by the current maintainer during the BDFN phase, or by a supermajority of the steering committee thereafter. Every amendment is a commit whose message cites the reason.
